I started building scripts that would help with my day-to-day tasks: unlock an AD account here, generate a new log there, etc. I took a basic web-development class in college, so I began taking that up again, along with Javascript for logic and backend development. I started out basic, with a small email signup webapp, which then rolled over to this personal site, which is my second rendition of it.
